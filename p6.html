Project #6 Optical Illusion

Description

My project is based on the Scintillating Grid Illusion. Scintillating Grid Illusion is a simultaneous lightness contrast illusion. When a viewer gazes at the grid, the dots seem to flash from the background color to the original color of the dots. From the basic Scintillating Grid, I added the interactive element. When people press one of the keys of 'b', 'g', 'a', 'l', or 's', a constellation will appear according to its beginning letter. The flashing effect of the illusion will also be weaker because the viewer's gaze is focused more on the dots emphasized in the constellations. Press 'a' key = Aries Press 'b' key = Big Dipper Press 'g' key = Gemini Press 'l' key = Leo Press 's' key = Sagittarius

Process

I used 'for' in loops to create the grid, and designed the background color in deep blue and the dots in pale yellow to resemble the colors of the universe. I used if (keyIsPressed) in a loop with if(key=='') to assign the constellations to their according to keys.

Reflection

I think the project is interesting that it makes us not only create an optical illusion, but also try to combine it with other functions.
