Project #8 Autobiographical Game

Description

This is my autobiographical game made with p5.js. I present my experience of moving to different cities in childhood. The first scene is about a northern city that is really cold during the winter. I remember I loved playing with the snow at that time. The baby's face turns blue when she touches the large snowflakes. A player can move the baby using the left and right arrow keys. When the baby moves all the way to the right, she enters the second scene.

In the second scene, the baby grows up to a little girl, and she arrives in a city that is really hot and is famous for chili. The girl's eyes turn to fire when she touches any chili. The player can move the girl using the top and down arrow keys.
