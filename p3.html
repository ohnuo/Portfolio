Project #3 Face Generator

Description

This is an animated face generator I made by using variables in p5. First, I draw the first and the second faces on 400*400 grid templates. Then I determined which elements would be variations in my p5 sketch. The colors of the face and eyes and the scale of the eyes change every time when the mouse is pressed. The pupils and the mouth also grow bigger. The variations in colors and sizes are created by employing the "let", "random" and "if-else" statements.
