Project #5 Exquisite Corpse

Description

For my sketch, I incorporated the head designed by Subin, the body designed by Selena, and the legs that I drew. Subin's character is a monkey who comes from the jungle and is evolving into a human. Selen's character is Ellie from an alternate universe where everything is made of circles and ovals. The new character here is an alien that lives on a planet that has no light, so they have electricity running in their bodies.

Design Process

I created functions for each body part and called the functions under function draw(). I used changeDirection to make the eyeballs move around. I also used random to let the colors change.

Reflection

I think the project is pretty fun and it pushes me to use things like arc() and rotate(), that I seldomly use in my previous projects.
